Exam Rules:
  • All questions are multiple choice (MCQ).
    You will be given several options for each question. You must select only one answer that you think is correct.
  • Only one choice is correct.
    Even if multiple options seem correct, only one is officially considered correct and will earn you points.
  • Each question carries different marks.
    Some questions may be worth more than others. For example, easy questions might give 1 point, medium ones 2 points, and hard ones 3 or more points.
  • Answer quickly (ASAP).
    While there may not be a strict timer for each question, you are expected to answer promptly to avoid system timeout or delays.
  • Do not refresh or go back!
    If you refresh the page or press the back button, you may receive a new random question, and your previous attempt might be counted as used.
  • Questions appear in random order.
    To maintain fairness and prevent cheating, questions are shuffled. No two students will receive the same question in the same order.
  • Instant result after submission.
    Once you submit your exam, you will immediately see your score along with correct/incorrect answers if enabled.
  • No option to go back to previous question.
    Once you move to the next question, you cannot return to the previous one. So make sure your answer is final before submitting.
  • Unanswered questions are treated as wrong.
    If you skip a question or close the exam without submitting, it will be considered incorrect and no points will be given.
  • One attempt only.
    You can only take the test once. Retaking the exam is not allowed unless the instructor grants special permission.
